Milestone 4 · Variable-length batching & padding

Measure what real, mixed-length protein batches cost. The baseline used same-length batches with zero padding by construction; this milestone introduces padding waste as a first-class, quantified metric.

What this milestone will do

Plan

  • Define padding waste. Split throughput into padded compute tokens/sec vs real amino-acid tokens/sec, and report the fraction of compute spent on pad tokens.
  • Batching strategies. Compare naive padding-to-max against length-aware approaches (e.g. length bucketing / sorting) on realistic length distributions.
  • Realistic inputs. Move beyond uniform-random lengths toward distributions that resemble real proteomes, since length statistics drive padding overhead.
Planned outputs

Artifacts

  • src/esm2_perf/batching.py — batching / bucketing utilities.
  • scripts/03_bench_batching.py — padding-efficiency benchmark.
  • A timestamped batching CSV under results/raw/.
